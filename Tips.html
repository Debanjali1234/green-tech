Recycling Tips

Recycling helps to reduce waste and conserve resources. Here are some tips:

  • Separate your recyclables (plastic, paper, glass) into designated bins.
  • Reuse containers or donate items that are still usable.
  • Avoid single-use plastics and opt for eco-friendly alternatives.

Behavioral Changes

Small changes in our daily habits can make a big difference:

  • Dispose of plastics in designated bins to prevent pollution.
  • Reduce plastic usage by carrying reusable bags and containers.
  • Participate in community clean-up drives to keep our environment clean.
Did you know?

In India, approximately 40% of plastics are disposed of improperly, contributing to environmental pollution.

Impact of Pollution

Pollution affects various aspects of our environment:

  • Loss of biodiversity due to habitat destruction.
  • Damage to agricultural lands and reduced crop yields.
  • Pollution-related health issues affecting humans and wildlife.
